Additional info

Built in 1909, Boulevard is one of the most beautiful and sightseeing places in Baku. The moment you set foot on Boulevard - which is 3 km 700 meters long - its unique aroma will take your breath away. You relax in the restaurants where the amusement attractions are located. The view from the 2nd largest Boulevard of Europe on the sea will amaze you. You will also encounter music fountains that have no analogues in the world.
